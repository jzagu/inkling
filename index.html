How to Play

Connect the start word to the target word by changing 1 or 2 letters at a time.

Each word you enter must be a valid English word.

Example
MORNING start
↓ change R→A
MOANING
↓ change O→E
MEANING
↓ change M→L
LEANING target reached!
  • You may change 1 or 2 letters per step.
  • Every word must be a valid Scrabble dictionary word.
  • You cannot reuse a word already in your chain.
  • You win when your word is 1 or 2 letters away from the goal — you don't need to type it exactly.
  • Reach the goal in par steps or fewer to earn a badge.
  • Use Undo to remove your last word.

A new puzzle is available every day at midnight Pacific Time.
